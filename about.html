About

A practical builder who likes clean systems.

I grew up in Knoxville, Tennessee. I started college as an engineering major at the University of Delaware, where an early computer science course pulled me toward software and data.

I transferred back to the University of Tennessee, Knoxville and graduated in December 2022 with a B.S. in Business Analytics. In July 2023, I moved to Philadelphia to pursue an M.S. in Data Science at Drexel University.

While finishing grad school, I joined NeuroFlow as a Data Engineer Associate in 2024. I completed my master’s in June 2025 and now work as a Data Engineer building scalable data infrastructure and analytics systems in healthcare.
